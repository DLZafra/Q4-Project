The Philippines is an archipelago with 7,000 islands. It is only natural for many places and tourist spots to be seen in the Philippines. The Philippines is such a nice place to explore whether you're a tourist or even a fellow filipino. Even the people who live in the Philippines aren't able to explore most of the country due to how big it is. Here are the top 3 places to visit in the Philippines.

Intramuros

This walled city is a sanctuary to the capital's iconic and historic sites constructed during the Spanish colonization of the Philippines. It is also among the most visited tourist spots in the Philippines. Many tourists visit Intramuros to grasp the past and see centuries-old institutions because of their old-world appeal

Vigan City

  • Vigan city is known for it's historical architecture
  • Vigan city is located in West Coast of Luzon

Magellan's Cross

  • Magellan's Cross is an historical site in Cebu, Mactan.
  • The history of Magellan reverts back to when they first discovered the Philippines
